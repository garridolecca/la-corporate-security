LA County Corporate Security Dashboard

343,000 incidents from 7 intelligence sources · Aug 2023 – Feb 2025

How to Use

  • Move your cursor over the map to activate the lens. A radial bar chart will appear showing the breakdown of incidents within the lens radius.
  • Scroll to zoom in and out of the map. Click and drag to pan.
  • Category filter (bottom bar) — isolate specific incident types: Armed Violence, Threats, Fire & Hazmat, Infrastructure, or Transport.
  • Data source filter (top-right bar) — filter by intelligence provider.
  • Radius selector (left bar) — adjust the lens area from 1 to 10 miles.
  • The radial bars around the lens show incident counts per category, broken down by year (darker = 2023, lighter = 2025). Longer bars mean more incidents.
